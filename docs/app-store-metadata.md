## Long Description
Resistor helps you understand your habits by tracking the moments that matter — when temptation strikes.

Instead of counting streaks or grading your days, Resistor logs each moment of temptation with a single tap. Over time, you see patterns: which times of day are hardest, which days of the week, and whether you're resisting more often than you think.

**How it works:**
- Create habits you're working on (sugar, smoking, impulse spending, or anything else)
- When temptation hits, open the app and tap Log
- Optionally record the outcome, intensity, and context
- Check Insights to see your trends and patterns

**What makes Resistor different:**
- No streaks — a single slip doesn't erase your progress
- No motivational quotes or gamification
- No social features — this is private
- No notifications — temptation isn't scheduled

**Your data stays yours:**
- Syncs across your devices with iCloud
- Export your data as JSON at any time
- No analytics, no tracking, no accounts
- Delete everything whenever you want

Resistor is a tool, not a coach. It shows you what's happening so you can decide what to do about it.
